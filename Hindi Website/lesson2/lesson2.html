Present Tense

There's no time like the present. In order to live in the present moment while speaking in Hindi, we will first have to learn about the infinitive form of some common words we use in Hindi. The infinitive form of a verb is the most basic form of the verb that isn't altered by any tenses (e.g. past, present, or future). In Hindi, the infinitive form of a verb contains the verb stem appended by "na". After viewing the infinitive form of a few basic verbs in Hindi, we will go over the present tense in all of its forms for those same words. Let's start with the words eat, sleep, and drink, since those are some of the most basic things you need to do to survive.


But before that, we must go into something even more fundamental. We must go into all the conjugations of the word "honaa" which means "to be" or "to exist" in Hindi. After all, there is nothing more fundamental than existence itself. If you're wondering what conjugations are, they just tell us how words interact with certain pronouns. A couple of examples in English would include "I am", "you are", "we are", "they are", and so on. Examples are listed in the conjugation table below.


Conjugation Table

Notice that there are straight lines at the end of each sentence shown below. They are called "dandas."
I am. Main hoon. मैँ हुँ। We are. Ham hain. हम हैं।
You are. Tu hai. तु है।  ⓘ The "tu" form should not be used in daily conversation with those you aren't close with. You are. Tum ho. तुम हो।
He/she is. Vah/yah hai. वह/यह है। They are. Ve/ye hain. वे/ये हैं।

To Function in Life

To Eat - Khaana

I eat. Main khaata hoon. मैँ खाता हुँ। We eat. Ham khaate hain. हम खाते हैं।
You eat. Tu khaata hai. तु खाता है। You eat. Tum khaate ho. तुम खाते हो।
He/she eats. Vah/yah khaata hai. वह/यह खाता है। They eat. Ve/ye khaate hain. वे/ये खाते हैं।

To Drink - Pinaa

I drink. Main pita hoon. मैँ पीता हुँ। We drink. Ham pite hain. हम पीते हैं।
You drink. Tu pita hai. तु पीता है। You eat. Tum pite ho. तुम पीते हो।
He/she eats. Vah/yah pita hai. वह/यह पीता है। They eat. Ve/ye pite hain. वे/ये पीते हैं।

To Sleep - Sonaa

I sleep. Main sota hoon. मैँ सोता हुँ। We sleep. Ham sote hain. हम सोते हैं।
You sleep. Tu sota hai. तु सोता है। You sleep. Tum sote ho. तुम सोते हो।
He/she sleep. Vah/yah sota hai. वह/यह सोता है। They sleep. Ve/ye sote hain. वे/ये सोते हैं।
